Greenbench

By Nachos Royale

Our goal:
By measuring the performance on your phone we can tell you if you should upgrade your phone or not.

When a new OS arrives for your phone, a lot of users feel that their phones becomes slower or sluggish. We measure the performance on a wide range of devices and give you the information, so you can make an informed decision on whether or not to upgrade. By helping you keep your phone fast and responsive, our hope is that you'll keep your phone a year or two longer. Keeping your phone longer reduces electronic waste and contributes to creating a more sustainable electronics industry.
